NetIQ

Vietnam's specialist in Organisational Network Analysis and AI strategy

NetIQ was co-founded by Dr. Duy Dang-Pham to translate cutting-edge network-science research into practical solutions for Vietnamese and Southeast Asian organisations. We help leadership teams understand the real — not the org-chart — structure of their organisations, and use those insights to design more effective AI adoption strategies.

Services

⬡

ONA Consulting & Diagnostics

Map your organisation's real relationship networks

We design and administer multiplex ONA surveys across five network layers — Work Advice, AI Support, Trust, Information, and Collaboration — then use network-science algorithms to identify key brokers, silos, and high-centrality nodes. Deliverables include interactive network visualisations, a diagnostic report, and a leadership briefing with prioritised recommendations.

◈

AI Champion Program Design

Identify and empower your internal AI advocates

Using betweenness centrality and Infomap community detection on your ONA data, we identify Oracle, Broker, and Silo-Buster archetypes and design a tailored AI champion program — including selection criteria, onboarding, activation playbooks, and progress-measurement frameworks.

◲

AI Strategy Training for Executives

Build leadership fluency in AI across three stages

Our executive education programme covers AI readiness assessment, strategic planning for AI adoption, and implementation governance — contextualised for Vietnamese and Southeast Asian regulatory, cultural, and competitive environments. Available as in-person workshops or blended formats.

◉

Research & Advisory Services

Independent research support and strategic advisory

We support organisations, government agencies, and academic institutions with independent research design, data collection, network analysis, and interpretation. Services include custom ONA study design, literature synthesis, policy advisory, and co-authorship on applied research projects.

Our methodology

NetIQ's ONA engagements follow a structured six-step process grounded in the academic research of Dr. Dang-Pham and validated across Vietnamese and international organisations.

  1. 01
    Needs Assessment

    Stakeholder interviews and organisational context mapping to define the right ONA scope and questions.

  2. 02
    Survey Design & Administration

    Validated multiplex ONA survey instruments administered across target populations, with anonymisation protocols.

  3. 03
    Network Analysis

    Betweenness centrality, degree centrality, Infomap community detection, and multiplex correlation analysis applied to clean relational data.

  4. 04
    Interpretation & Reporting

    Interactive visualisations, executive summary, diagnostic report, and leadership workshop to interpret findings and plan actions.

  5. 05
    Program Design & Implementation

    Design of AI champion programs, intervention plans, or structural changes informed by ONA findings.

  6. 06
    Follow-up & Measurement

    Repeat ONA assessment (6–12 months) to measure network change and evaluate program impact.
