NetIQ

Vietnam's specialist in Organisational Network Analysis for change-driven organisations

Behind every change initiative — whether it is an AI rollout, a leadership restructure, a DEI programme, or a post-merger integration — there is a human network that will determine whether it succeeds or fails. Organisational Network Analysis makes that network visible. NetIQ was founded by Dr. Duy Dang-Pham to bring the rigour of academic network science to Vietnamese and Southeast Asian organisations navigating exactly that challenge. Our deepest expertise lies in AI adoption, digital transformation, and information security — but ONA works wherever relationships, trust, and informal influence shape whether change takes hold.

Why NetIQ

Academic rigour

Our methods have been tested, challenged, and refined across 70+ peer-reviewed publications — not assembled from a proprietary playbook. When we tell you something about your organisation's network, the science supports it.

Broad application, deep expertise

ONA can be applied wherever human networks shape organisational outcomes — leadership, communication, DEI, knowledge management, and change management of any kind. Our deepest research expertise lies in AI adoption, digital transformation, and information security, but the diagnostic methodology is universal.

One team, start to finish

You don't manage multiple vendors or translate findings into action yourself. We take you from scoping question to implemented programme in a single continuous engagement — with one team accountable throughout.

Actionable outputs

Every engagement closes with specific, prioritised actions your leadership team can begin implementing within weeks — not a report that answers one question and raises three more.
