Research

Research focus

My work sits at the intersection of network science, organisational behaviour, and applied AI — producing actionable frameworks for practitioners and contributions to top IS venues.

⬡

Organisational Network Analysis

Using multiplex network methods — Work Advice, AI Support, Trust, Information, and Collaboration ties — to reveal the invisible architecture of how organisations actually function.

Learn about ONA
◈

AI Champion Programs

Identifying Oracle, Broker, and Silo-Buster archetypes within organisations using betweenness centrality and community-detection algorithms to drive grass-roots AI adoption.

Learn about AI Champions
◉

Information Security Governance

Examining how individuals, teams, and organisations protect digital assets — from end-user security behaviour to enterprise-level governance frameworks in Vietnamese and ASEAN contexts.

Explore research
◲

Digital Transformation

Studying how organisations navigate large-scale technology change — change management, knowledge transfer, technology acceptance, and the social dynamics underpinning successful transformation.
