## general bio
### one-paragraph:
Dr. Duy Dang-Pham is an expert in the field of Management Information Systems (MIS), with over 10 years of experience in conducting research and teaching topics related to information security management, digital transformation, and the application of digital technologies in organizational management. He has directly designed and delivered numerous training programs, in-depth workshops, and seminars for management-level participants across a wide range of organizations and industries — including PPJ Group, Vietcombank, EVN Finance, and TTT Corporation — as well as for local government agencies to support businesses and communities in the digital transformation process. Dr. Duy Dang-Pham is the co-author of over 60 peer-reviewed research publications in reputable international journals and conferences. He has led or participated in research projects with a total funding amount exceeding USD 341,500 (as of 2023). In 2024, he was awarded the title of Fulbright U.S. Scholar-in-Residence at Gonzaga University, USA — a recognition of his significant contributions to international research and education. He is also the co-founder of the Vietnam Association of Information Systems (VAIS), where he actively promotes academic collaboration and the practical application of MIS in Vietnam’s rapidly evolving technological landscape.

## academic bio
### one-paragraph:
Dr. Duy Dang-Pham is a Senior Lecturer and Associate Head of Department (Research) at The Business School, RMIT University Vietnam, where he leads research initiatives, fosters a collaborative research culture, and mentors academic staff and PhD candidates. His expertise spans Management Information Systems (MIS), with a focus on information security behaviors and management, digital transformation, and emerging digital technologies. Prior to his current role, he served as HDR Coordinator & Delegated Authority, managing six PhD programs across business disciplines, and as Senior Program Manager for the Bachelor of IT and Bachelor of Software Engineering programs. Dr. Dang-Pham has co-authored over 60 publications in internationally recognized journals and conferences and has secured over US$ 341,500 in research funding. In 2024, he was honored as a Fulbright U.S. Scholar-in-Residence at Gonzaga University, USA, and he is also a Co-founder of the Vietnam Chapter of the Association for Information Systems (VAIS).
