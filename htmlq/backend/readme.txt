INSTALLATION

(1) Open the exe.php-file in any text editor.
(2) Change the EXPORT_MAIL (line 13) to your 
    personal e-mail address. 
(3) Upload all files to your web server.
(4) Make sure, that the folder "data" is 
    writable for PHP.
(5) Modify the submitUrl in your FlashQ 
    config-file.
